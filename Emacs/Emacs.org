* Emacs 安装 Go语言的支持
  http://sachachua.com/blog/2011/01/emacs-24-package-manager/
  添加如下代码进入.emacs
  (require 'package)
  (add-to-list 'package-archives
      '("elpa". "http://tromey.com/elpa/"))
  (add-to-list 'package-archives
      '("marmalade"."http://marmalade-repo.org/packages/"))

  之后M-x: package-list-packages搜索go-mode, 点击安装即可
